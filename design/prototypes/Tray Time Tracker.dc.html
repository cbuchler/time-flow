Cross-platform tray app · interactive prototype

One time-tracker, four native shells

A menu-bar / system-tray app for time tracking and Pomodoro. The popover is the window — every secondary screen is a drill-down inside the same fixed footprint. One shared component system, recolored by semantic tokens, rendered to each platform's desktop HIG in light and dark. The macOS variant follows Menu Bar Extra, NSPopover, AppKit sheet, and Liquid Glass material conventions rather than iOS navigation patterns.
